# Rangyul Group of Hotels

Welcome to the official repository for the **Rangyul Group of Hotels** website. This project showcases a premium hospitality brand located in Kargil, Ladakh, offering a collection of properties designed for comfort, luxury, and authentic Ladakhi experiences.

## ğŸ¨ Project Overview

This website serves as the digital storefront for the Rangyul Group, allowing visitors to explore their three main properties, view special offers, and easily make bookings via WhatsApp. The design focuses on responsiveness, visual appeal, and user-friendly navigation.

### Our Properties

1.  **Hotel Rangyul (Town Property):** Located in Baroo, Kargil. Ideal for families and transit travelers, offering easy access to the market and local attractions.
2.  **Rangyul Resort (Resort Experience):** Located in Suru Valley, Kargil. A premium cottage-style resort surrounded by mountains, perfect for nature lovers and honeymooners.
3.  **Galaxy Hotel (Premium Stay):** Located in Kargil Center. A value-for-money option preferred by tour operators and groups, offering spacious rooms and modern facilities.

## âœ¨ Features

-   **Responsive Design:** Fully responsive layout that adapts to mobile, tablet, and desktop screens.
-   **Interactive Hero Slider:** A dynamic image slider on the homepage to showcase the beauty of Ladakh.
-   **Property Showcases:** Individual pages and sliders for each hotel property.
-   **Interactive Map:** Integrated Leaflet map to show the exact location of each property in Kargil.
-   **Quick Booking System:** A modal-based booking form that redirects users to WhatsApp with their booking details pre-filled.
-   **Premium Offers:** A section highlighting special deals for different types of travelers (Bikers, Groups, Seasonal).
-   **Scroll Animations:** Smooth scroll animations and a progress bar to enhance user engagement.
-   **Mobile Menu:** A hamburger menu for easy navigation on smaller devices.
