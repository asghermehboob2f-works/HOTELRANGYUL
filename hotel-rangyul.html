ABOUT HOTEL RANGYUL

Experience the perfect blend of modern luxury and traditional Ladakhi hospitality at Hotel Rangyul.

Located in the heart of Kargil, Hotel Rangyul offers premium accommodation with stunning mountain views. Our hotel is designed to provide guests with maximum comfort and convenience during their stay in Ladakh.

With modern amenities, spacious rooms, and exceptional service, we ensure every guest enjoys a memorable experience in the Himalayan region.
