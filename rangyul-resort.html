ABOUT RANGYUL RESORT

Experience the tranquility of Ladakh while enjoying modern comforts in our eco-friendly resort. Perfect for nature lovers, families, and adventure seekers.

Rangyul Resort is designed for travelers who seek both adventure and relaxation. Wake up to the sound of birds and breathtaking mountain views. Spend your day exploring nature trails or simply relax in our green surroundings.

Our resort combines sustainable practices with luxury amenities, ensuring an unforgettable Himalayan experience that respects the natural environment.
